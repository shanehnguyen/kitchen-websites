Privacy.

The short version: I don’t sell your data, and I don’t share it with anyone except the tools I need to run this site and reply to you.

What I collect

When you fill out a form or email me, I collect what you give me: name, email, website URL, and whatever you write in the message. I use it to reply to you. Nothing else.

This site uses standard web analytics (anonymous aggregate traffic numbers, no personal identifiers) and Calendly for scheduling when you book a call. Calendly has its own privacy policy.

What I don’t do

  • I don’t sell your information to anyone.
  • I don’t run retargeting or behavioural advertising pixels.
  • I don’t add you to a newsletter unless you explicitly opt in.
  • I don’t share your project details or site content with other clients.

Cookies

This site uses the minimum cookies needed to make forms work and to remember if you’ve dismissed the floating schedule chip. No tracking cookies.

How to reach us about your data
