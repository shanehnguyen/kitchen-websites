## v2.1 DIRECTION CHANGE — read this first
The frame moved from "stop your referrals leaking" to **"get more customers, period."** The hero, the letter, and the mechanism name above reflect the new direction. The mechanism is now **The Found & Chosen System** (get found by homeowners already searching, get chosen once they find you), which keeps "more customers" honest and verifiable instead of drifting into the burned word, "leads."

**Still to propagate (next pass):** the three offer-page leads (Rescue/Rebuild/Run) below still open from the homeowner's point of view and lean on the old referral frame. They need the same him-first rewrite: open on what *he's* living and what he's tried, then bring in the found/chosen mechanic. The offer terms, stacks, guarantees, and FAQs do not change.

## WHAT I TOOK FROM THE KING KONG OFFERS, AND WHAT I LEFT ON THE TABLE

**Took (the mechanics that make their offers irresistible):**
- Results in advance. You see the work before you pay.
- "You pay nothing unless you win." Risk fully on me.
- The value stack with dollar amounts, totaled, then a price that lands far under the total.
- A reason-why for the generosity, so there's no "what's the catch."
- Qualification framed as a gate. "Three things must be true before you book."
- Scarcity with a real reason behind it.
- One offer, one page, one argument, one CTA.

**Left (the two things that would burn you specifically):**
- **Their results guarantee** ("beat your results 30% or we work free"). That's a performance/ranking promise. Google flags it, the FTC chased it, and it's the exact shape of the promise that burned your buyer. Your guarantees ride on what you control: the deadline, the deliverables, the tracked number. Same risk reversal, none of the landmine.
- **The tiger-blood hype voice** ("blow your umbilicus clean off," "$1.33 billion," "sexy salesperson"). King Kong sells to entrepreneurs who already buy marketing. You sell to a 55-year-old who's been robbed twice and is scanning for the next con. Same structure, your voice, zero hype.
- **And the proof wall.** King Kong's offer is backed by a thousand case studies. You have none yet. Every proof slot below is marked `{{ }}` and waits for a real one. The verified market stats carry the load until your first Rescue lands.
