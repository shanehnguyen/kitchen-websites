## 2. THE 10 QUESTIONS (with answer weights)

Score = sum of all questions. Max 100. Each answer maps to points AND to segment flags.

**Q1. How many Google reviews does your business have right now?** *(max 12)*
- 50+ → 12 · 21–50 → 9 · 6–20 → 6 · 1–5 → 2 [INVISIBLE flag] · Zero → 0 [INVISIBLE flag]

**Q2. How many does your biggest local competitor have?** *(max 8)*
- Fewer than me → 8 · About the same → 5 · More → 2 [RIVALRY flag] · Way more → 0 [RIVALRY flag] · No idea → 2 [INVISIBLE flag]

**Q3. Who controls the login to your Google Business Profile?** *(max 10)*
- Me/my team → 10 · An old marketing guy → 2 [BURNED flag] · No idea → 0 [INVISIBLE flag] · What login? → 0 [INVISIBLE flag]

**Q4. When did you last add photos of a finished job to your Google profile?** *(max 10)*
- This month → 10 · This year → 6 · Can't remember → 2 [SLAMMED flag] · Never → 0 [INVISIBLE flag]

**Q5. How fast does your business respond to a new inquiry?** *(max 12)*
- Within the hour → 12 · Same day → 8 · Next day → 3 [SLAMMED flag] · When we get to it → 0 [SLAMMED flag]

**Q6. Where does most of your work come from?** *(max 8)*
- Mix of referrals + online → 8 · Referrals/repeat only → 4 [LEAK-PRIME flag] · Lead platforms (Angi/Houzz) → 2 [BURNED flag] · Honestly not sure → 2

**Q7. Have you ever paid for marketing that didn't produce?** *(max 10)*
- Never tried paid marketing → 6 · Once → 6 [BURNED flag] · More than once → 3 [BURNED flag] · Don't get me started → 0 [BURNED-STRONG flag]
*(Note: "never tried" scores 6 not 10 — inexperience isn't capture-readiness.)*

**Q8. Does your website show your actual work?** *(max 10)*
- My real projects → 10 · A mix with stock photos → 5 · Mostly stock → 2 · What website? → 0 [INVISIBLE flag]

**Q9. If a homeowner searched "kitchen remodeler near me" (or "kitchen showroom near me") tonight, would you be in the top 3 map results?** *(max 12)*
- Yes, consistently → 12 · Sometimes → 7 · No → 2 [RIVALRY flag] · Never checked → 0 [INVISIBLE flag]

**Q10. How's lead flow compared to two years ago?** *(max 8)*
- Better → 8 · Same → 6 · Slower → 2 [SLAMMED/URGENT flag] · Way slower → 0 [URGENT flag]

## 4. SEGMENT LOGIC

Assign ONE primary segment (priority order):
1. **BURNED** — if Q7 = "don't get me started," OR 2+ BURNED flags → burned email sequence
2. **INVISIBLE** — if 3+ INVISIBLE flags → invisible sequence
3. **SLAMMED** — if 2+ SLAMMED flags → slammed sequence
4. Default (no strong flags) → INVISIBLE sequence (safest general fit)
URGENT flag (Q10 slow/way slower) = priority tag in CRM: these leads get called first.

**Score bands:**
- **0–39: CRITICAL LEAK** — "Your referrals are checking Google and finding your competitor."
- **40–69: LEAKING** — "You're losing a real share of the homeowners who already chose you."
- **70–100: CAPTURE-READY** — "You're ahead of most of the industry — here's what separates you from the top 3."

## 7. BUILD NOTES
- **Tooling:** ScoreApp-class tool = weekend build; custom build = the portfolio flex (do v2 custom; launch on the fast path).
- **Tracking events:** ScoreStarted · ScoreComplete (Meta optimization event) · GraderRequested · CallBooked (true north).
- **Congruence:** every cold ad's promise ("see how you stack up") is answered verbatim by this page's headline. Burn-angle ads enter via the same page — Block C's burned close does the catching.
- **Tone law:** the word "quiz" appears nowhere. It's a "check." Blue-collar verbs throughout.
- **Honesty law:** percentile language stays soft ("roughly") — it's an estimate from the YDOP benchmarks, not a measured percentile. Never imply measurement that didn't happen.
