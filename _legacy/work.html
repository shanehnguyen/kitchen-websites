The build

Three systems, one launch.

The homeowner storefront, the contractor portal, and the product pages, shipped together under one fixed price. Here’s what each layer actually does.

01 · Storefront

A storefront built to close on the first visit.

Editorial hero, style showcase, full product-page anatomy with configurator and cross-sell, and a 3-step design-consult form. Same dealer, same catalog, dealer-quality presentation.

  • Editorial hero with Premium Series positioning
  • Guarantee row: 3-day ship, best price, 5-year warranty, 30-day returns
  • Designer spotlight + project gallery for the browse-first homeowner
02 · Contractor portal

Self-serve quotes. Tag-gated pricing. Recurring revenue.

A qualifying application filters serious pros from tire-kickers. Approved contractors get a tag that unlocks tier pricing catalog-wide, and the orders view above is what they log into. Homeowners never see the discount.

  • Orders dashboard with total orders, spend, and amount saved
  • Filterable order table with project tags, status, and invoice flows
  • Quick-quote tool, project folders, and saved payment methods
03 · Product pages

Filterable catalog. 40+ door styles. One click each.

The collection page above shows the metafield filter system in production: product type, collection, finish, price, all faceted live. Cabinets that used to live in PDFs now sit on the catalog page with renders, prices, and configuration in reach.

  • Filter facets for product type, collection, finish, and price
  • Product cards with renders, configuration, and price visibility
  • Spec table, reviews, Q&A, and cross-sell on every PDP
